Yog

Description

This yogurt is cashew-based with no added flavor. I put berries in it or sliced fruit suchs as pears. I also add nuts, grains, etc.

Ingredients

  • Cashew Yogurt
  • Berries or other Fruit
  • Nuts, Grains, etc.

Steps

  1. Pour yogurt into bowl
  2. Mix in berries, fruit, nuts, grains, etc.
  3. Enjoy!
